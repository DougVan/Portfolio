Bio

Maggie is 3.5 yrs old in the photograph above and is a rescue from South Carolina. She is the happiest dog I have ever met and brings a lot of joy into our home. Maggie is a mixed breed, Husky, Lab and German Shepherd. Very close to an Alaskin Husky. Lots and lots of energy.

Favorite Activities:

  • Playing Ball:
    • Retrieving the ball.
    • Chasing the ball into giant leaf piles.
    • Playing ball in the ocean.
    • Dramatically crashing through drifts while playing ball in the snow.

  • Hiking:
    • Maggie loves to hike and sniff and is like my uphill motor. Going down a slope I ask her to "go easy" and she slows down. What a good dog.

  • Riding In The Car:
    • Sniffing the air.
    • Seeing the sites.
    • Getting cookies at the bank and pet store.

  • Playing with her BFF Bail.

  • Chewing on sticks:
    • Nothing like taking big sticks and making them into little sticks. Seems like this can only be done on the lawn and not in the wood.

  • Digging Holes:
    • Holes in the lawn.
    • Holes in the woods.
    • Holes on the beach.

  • Indoor Activities:
    • Chewing on her stuffies.
    • Tug of war with her stuffies.
    • Moving all if her stuffies from one room to another, this may include socks, shoes, glooves and towels.
